Laptops

Posted on april 20th

In the market there are a lot of good laptops available at the moment, the two main operating systems are Windows and MacOS.

Windows

The vast majority of laptops available at the moment come with Windows operating system. There are gaming laptops, such as lenovo's legion 7 and razer blade 15; there are also ultrabooks like Dell's XPS15.

The CPU is like the motor of the laptop, there are two major brands: Intel and AMD . I think the main thing in which you have to focus when buying a laptop is the CPU, that it will be capable enough to make the tasks that you need.

MacOS

MacOS, as already you may know, is only available in Apple products. It is a very clean OS. There are two main Apple laptops: The MacBook Pro, which is the flagship and the MacBook Air, which is a more simple, less expensive variant.

Also, in this laptops, the CPU is very important. Until 2020 apple laptops have been using Intel's CPUs. From 2021 they will start to use Apple CPUs . They are generating a lot of high expecatitves in tech enthusiasts, like us.

Smartphones

Posted on june 9th

There are two big main categories in the smartphone segment. The flagships and the budget phones

Flagships

Flagship phones are the most expensive, and top notch that a brand can bring to the public. For Samsung they are the Note 20 ultra and the galaxy fold (which I think it mainly belongs to other category, but this will be adressed on a future post) for apple it is the iPhone 11 Pro and for OnePlus it is the OnePlus 8 Pro.

The main characteristic of a flagship phone is that they have the highest end technology. For example a Snapdragon 865+ as a processor (in the case of the iPhone a A13 bionic chip), a high refresh rate screen - 90 hz to 120 hz, a good battery life and a top notch camera. As the years pass by the price of this type of phones has been increasing rapidly. Today they are on the $1,000 price range. They are who generate the most margin for their companies.

Budget Phones

Budget phones, has been recently a tight fight between the phone companies. Keeping "budget kings" aside ("kings" are Xiaomi, Oppo and Huawei). The big companies have launched this year some really good competitors for the $350 - $400 price range.

Brand Phone Name
Apple iPhone SE
Google Pixel 4a
OnePlus OnePlus Nord

The challenge at this price point is to deliver a good phone which is within the budget. Here companies do not make money from a big margin, instead they profit form high volume sales.
